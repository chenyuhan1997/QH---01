# :wrench::wrench::wrench: QH-01

Since I began studying hardware design in 2016, I have had a dream of researching portable mech suits. Traditional research on robotic arms or mechs is often extraordinarily expensive. I hope to design robotic arms similar to those of Doctor Octopus at a minimal cost, equipped with only a few sensors such as cameras, and integrated with vision and auditory large language model capabilities to interact with me. This is a highly, highly idealistic project.
